FAQs:
  • How do I start training? Our training materials are open and available to anyone. Simply start reading this digital book, starting at the INTRO chapter.
  • How do I get an official MetaCoders certification? Usually, this is by invitation only -- i.e. we've hired you as a MetaCoders coach. We'll assess your knowledge, grade your submissions, coach you along the way, and grant you a certification at the end.
  • How do I submit my assessments to MetaCoders If you've been accepted as a MetaCoders coach-in-training, you will upload your assessment video to YouTube and email a link to your Training Lead.
  • How do I get a certification if I'm not employed by MetaCoders? You'll need to contact us at training@metacoders.org. We occasionally make exceptions for enthusiastic people who are trying to bring MetaCoders to their city and are training in advance of an official application.
