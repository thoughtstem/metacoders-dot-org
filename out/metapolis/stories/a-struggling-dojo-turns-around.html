    A Struggling Dojo Turns Around

    Place
    Kick Butt Martial Arts
    Time
    Tue, January 5, 2021 11:00 AM-4:00 AM
    Characters
    • Johnny Lawrence

    Outline...


    • A local martial arts dojo who is struggling.
    • Owner, Johnny Lawrence, wants to rent out space to other organizations to help him make rent.
    • He reaches out to MetaCoders and sets up a class with the Program Manager, Sandy.
    • After MetaCoders has been running classes in the evenings for a few classes, some of the kids who join the coding class also join the dojo for martial arts classes, which helps his business even more.
