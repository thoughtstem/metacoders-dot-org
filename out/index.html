Meta Stories

A good education takes a village.

Coding education isn’t possible in many communities because the expertise isn’t there yet. We help train community members to bring grass-roots coding education to local kids.

We’re teaching coding to save the world.

Computer scientists saved millions of lives in WWII. That trend continues today. In labs around the world, computer scientists are tackling cancer, climate change, and terrorism. If we are going to save the world, we need more people coding.
