What Your Donations Support


Financial aid to help all students have the opportunity to learn how to code no matter their financial means.
Customized Chromebooks filled with MetaCoders curriculum and programming languages for students to use during classes and camps.
Reduced overall costs of classes and camps for all families.
Classroom supplies and learning materials that help keep students engaged. This includes things like badges to earn, toys to earn, kata cards, and more!

Other Ways to Donate


At this time, MetaCoders primarily accepts monetary donations to support our efforts in computer science education. There are a few other ways you can donate besides through the donation form above:

Donate by Mail

Mail a check to our headquarters:

2635 Camino del Rio South, Ste 103, San Diego, CA 92108

Please include your return mailing address and phone number so that we can send you a donation receipt.

Donate by Phone
Call us at 858-869-9430 with payment details and we can take your donation over the phone.
Donate by Email
If you have a non-monetary donation, please email us at contact@metacoders.org to see if we are able to accept your donation.
